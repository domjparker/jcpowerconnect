Services


JC Power Connect provides solar energy consultation services to different types of clients, including the design, installation, legal and financial advisory services for clients. Additionally, for those clients who can not qualify for solar, alternative measures on how to save on gas and electricity bills will be provided.

Commerical


JC Power Connect can help commercial clients convert to solar energy. JC Power Connect is responsible for the design and installation of solar panels and will advise on different financing options (ie purchase or leasing). ). All related legal notarizations and paperwork are handled by JC Power Connect, making this a seamless process for the client. Additionally, for those clients who are not qualified for solar, JC Power Connect will provide consultation on alternatives to save on gas and electricity.
