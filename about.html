Judy Cazeau LLC, JC Power Connect

Consulting Agency

JC Power Connect was founded by Judy Cazeau, a serial entrepreneur, real estate investor, and green energy advocate. With over a century of expertise in consulting, Judy began her journey as an independent marketing representative helping her clients reduce the cost of all their essential bills (utilities, gas, telecom and tele med). Judy is known amongst her friends, family, and business associates as a power connector. She has a natural passion for building and creating meaningful relationships, providing business connections and resources for her clients, hence the name JC Power Connect which makes her an ideal consultant when working with her.

She is a powerful networker and has cultivated an ecosystem of powerful connections. Judy’s leadership as a Director with the Business Network International, Inc. (BNI) for the past 4yrs, she has been able to support her 4 chapters grow in membership numbers, as well as help entrepreneurs, small business owners and corporate representatives close approximately $6M million of sales annually through her leadership. Judy has been able to take her expertise as a consultant to begin her business to help her clients invest in solar energy whether it be for their homes, businesses, or non-profits. She is passionate about educating her clients about the benefits of clean energy and how that can be combined with real estate.


As a licensed real estate professional the two are ever changing industries and Judy has kept herself at the forefront on continuing her education. Judy recently completed her Women’s In Entrepreneurship certification from Cornell University. Judy also used the time during the pandemic to complete her Commercial Real Estate and Investing Certificate from MIT. Judy is from Haitian decent and envisions being able to create green development projects that will help her country and others along in the Carribean. In her spare time, Judy loves to read books on entrepreneurship, leadership. She is a fitness enthusiast, loves to eat clean. One thing that Judy lives by is “Do everything with Integrity”!!
